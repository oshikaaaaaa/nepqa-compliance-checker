## SECTION 7 — OPEN ITEMS AND DISCREPANCIES

There are several discrepancies and missing data points that require clarification from the manufacturer. The power factor nominal and adjustable range differ between the SUN and CE series, with the CE series lacking confirmation of adjustability. Critical performance data such as inverter efficiency, MPPT efficiency, total harmonic distortion, anti-islanding (for CE series), and warranty terms are absent or incomplete. The CE series documentation is limited to a single safety test report and lacks other required certifications and technical datasheets.

The serial number labeling is not confirmed for either series, which is a mandatory NEPQA requirement. The SUN series and CE series appear to be related but distinct product lines, with the CE series manufactured by Zhejiang CHISAGE but produced at NingBo Deye’s factory, suggesting a close relationship but different branding and possibly different design focus.

Before submission, SunBridge should request missing certifications, detailed technical datasheets, warranty agreements, and confirmation of labeling completeness, especially serial number presence.
